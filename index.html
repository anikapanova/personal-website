Biography

I am Anika Panova, I am 19 years old and I am currently a student at the Faculty of Information Sciences and Computer Engineering (FINKI). I have focused my academic journey on the field of information science and technology, with a particular interest in creative and digital media.

In addition to my studies, I also work in marketing for companies, where I gain experience in content creation, digital strategy and branding. This allows me to combine my technical knowledge with creativity and communication skills.

In my free time, I enjoy drawing, as well as working with image editing in Photoshop and graphic design. Through the visual arts, I manage to combine my passion for art with skills from the digital world.

I strive for constant personal and professional development, I am open to new challenges and believe in the power of technology and design to create positive change. I am always open to new projects, collaborations and learning something new.

If you like what I do or if you have an idea for a joint project – feel free to contact me!
